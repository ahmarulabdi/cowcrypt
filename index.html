CowCrypt: MooTools encryption classes

CowCrypt provides popular hashing and encryption algorithms for the MooTools JavaScript framework, including AES, Twofish, SHA-1 and more. My goal is to provide much of the same robust functionality as the crypto-js library, but with the elegance of MooTools. CowCrypt is released under the MIT License.
